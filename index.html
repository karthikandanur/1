About Me

I am Karthik A N, a Ph.D. scholar in Agronomy at the University of Agricultural Sciences, Bangalore (OGPA: 8.778), specializing in nano fertilizer applications, weed management, and sustainable cropping systems. My doctoral research focuses on the effect of nano-fertilizers on growth and productivity of maize-greengram cropping system, contributing cutting-edge insights into precision nutrient management.

With a strong academic foundation — M.Sc. Agronomy (OGPA: 8.95) and B.Sc. Agriculture (OGPA: 8.10) — I have built an impressive publication portfolio spanning international journals, national journals, popular science articles, and book chapters. I am an ICAR-NET Qualified Agricultural Scientist (Roll No. 5054336391) and the recipient of multiple prestigious awards including the Young Agronomist Award 2026 and the Best M.Sc. Thesis Award 2024.

My research interests span nano-technology in agriculture, soil fertility management, climate-smart farming, weed science, and inter-cropping systems. I have actively contributed to teaching at UAS Bangalore through formal Teaching Assistantship and co-authored practical manuals for undergraduate agronomy courses.
